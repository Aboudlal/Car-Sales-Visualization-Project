## Attribute Definitions

### Manufacturer

**Description:** Brand name of the vehicle
**Type:** Categorical
**Example:** Ford, Toyota, BMW

---

### Model

**Description:** Specific vehicle model name
**Type:** Categorical
**Example:** Civic, Mustang, Corolla

---

### Unit Sales

**Description:** Total number of units sold
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** Vehicles

---

### Price ($)

**Description:** Original sale price of the vehicle
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** USD ($)

---

### Year Resale Value ($)

**Description:** Estimated resale value after one year
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** USD ($)

---

### Retention %

**Description:** Percentage of value retained after one year
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** Percentage (%)

---

### Retention Classification

**Description:** Grouped retention performance
**Type:** Categorical
**Values:** GOOD / POOR

---

### Horsepower

**Description:** Engine power output
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** HP

---

### HP Level

**Description:** Grouped horsepower category
**Type:** Categorical
**Values:** Low / Medium / High

---

### Vehicle Type

**Description:** Vehicle classification category
**Type:** Categorical
**Values:** Passenger / Car

---

### Fuel Efficiency (MPG)

**Description:** Fuel economy performance
**Type:** Numeric
**Unit:** Miles Per Gallon (MPG)

---

## Notes

Some technical attributes such as wheelbase, fuel capacity, and performance factors were removed during data cleaning to focus analysis on business-relevant insights.
